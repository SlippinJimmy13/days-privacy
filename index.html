Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Days is a birthday and anniversary reminder app. This policy explains what data the app collects — and why it's very short.

Your data stays on your device

Everything you enter into Days — names, dates, notes, reminder settings — is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to a server, never shared with third parties, and never leaves your phone.

No accounts

Days has no accounts, no sign-in, and no registration. There is nothing to log into.

No analytics or tracking

Days contains no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, and no crash reporting SDK. We do not know how you use the app, how often you open it, or anything else about your behaviour.

No internet connection required

Days works entirely offline. The app does not request internet permission and cannot make network connections.

Contacts

If you choose to import birthdays from your contacts, Days reads your contacts once to perform the import. It does not retain ongoing access to your contacts after that.

Backups

If you use the backup feature, Days saves a file to a location you choose on your own device. This file is under your control. Days does not have access to it after you save it.

Data collection

Days does not collect any data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
